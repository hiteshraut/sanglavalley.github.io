THINGS TO DO

Nature Walks

Ever wished to be one with nature? Ever thought of walking along mesmerizing views of mountains, alleys, rivers, orchards and more? Take nature walks in the Sangla valley and fulfil those golden fantasies. We organize nature walks for our guests in special Combo packages.

As you walk through the valley, the Cedars and the Pines, the serene views and gorgeous sceneries all around leave you gasping in awe. Catch some local sight-seeing in Sangla village, explore Chitkul, Baspa valley and around, while surrounded by spectacular snow-clad peaks.

Book Now
Trekking

Called “the most beautiful valley in the Himalayas, the 95 km stretch of Sangla valley, surrounded by mountains and dotted with Pines and Cedars, is a trekker’s dream coming true.

Choose from our special trekking packages where we take you to the hills, safe and secured.

Take the Sangla to Chitkul trek, via the Kamru fort or the Kinner Kailash trek, an adventurous climbing session. Let your adventure genes out and trek away, you’ll make memories that last a lifetime.

Book Now
Chitkul Trip

The last inhabited village on the Indo-Tibet border, lying deep within the Kinnaur valley, Chitkul is that dream destination, straight out of fantasies. From meadows to mountains, grasslands to windy landscapes, river valleys and more, this small town has everything. We organize special one-day trips to Chitkul under our special packages, letting you relish your trip, while we handle the rest.

Book Now
Hiking

A nature’s retreat away from humdrum of cities and monotony of the routine – hiking in Sangla valley is a rejuvenating experience. Through lush meadows, forested slopes and gorgeous valleys, admire the little wonders of nature as you walk along. We organize special hiking trips for our guests letting them experience the unforgettable glory of the Himalayas.

Choose from our packages and discover the hidden treasures within.

Rappelling and Rock Climbing

We arrange adventure activities like rappelling and rock climbing for those who love to go beyond the ordinary and test their limits.

Be it climbing big rocks, overcoming steep challenges and obstacles, or controlled descent down the rocks, safe and secured, we take care of all safety procedures and let you enjoy the thrill of the adventures.

Angling and Trout Fishing

It’s a one-of- a-kind experience when you angle for trouts in the crystal clear waters of the Baspa river in the Sangla valley. For the enthusiasts, it is a treat, a memorable experience to remember.
